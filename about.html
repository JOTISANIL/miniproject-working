Our Vision

At Kudumbashree Online, we envision a future where digital platforms enable inclusive and sustainable development for communities. By bringing Kudumbashree units online, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional community development programs and the opportunities presented by the digital age. Our mission is to empower individuals and families by providing them with a user-friendly online platform that facilitates the formation and functioning of Kudumbashree units. We strive to promote skill development, entrepreneurship, and economic independence among marginalized sections of society through digital literacy, training programs, and access to online marketplaces. We invite you to explore the Kudumbashree Online platform, where you can learn more about Kudumbashree units, their impact, and how you can get involved. Together, let's unlock the potential of technology to create a positive change in the lives of individuals and communities, one Kudumbashree unit at a time. Thank you for joining us on this journey!
